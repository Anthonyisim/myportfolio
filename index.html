About Me

I'm Chigazor, an IT operations professional turned software developer who loves transforming ideas into clean, functional, and human centered products. My journey started with a deep curiosity about how the web works, and today I bring that curiosity to life using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap 5 to build responsive, modern interfaces. I’m especially passionate about creating products that genuinely make life easier, tools that feel intuitive, solve real problems, and just work without hitches.

Before diving fully into development, I built a strong creative foundation as a graphic designer, a journey that began back in 2015. That experience trained my eye for detail, layout, color, and visual storytelling, and it still heavily influences how I approach software today. I don’t just think about how things function, I care deeply about how they feel to the user. This blend of design thinking and technical execution allows me to bridge the gap between aesthetics and usability.

My passion for problem solving has grown, so has my interest in backend and full-stack web development. I’m actively expanding my skill set by learning the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js), with a focus on building scalable, data-driven applications. I’m excited by the challenge of architecting systems behind the scenes and connecting them seamlessly to elegant frontends. For me, software development isn’t just a career, it’s a craft, and I’m committed to continuously learning, building, and pushing ideas from concept to reality as part of a team or freelancing.
